Our Story

Established in 2015, Navodaya Girls Hostel has been a trusted name in providing safe and comfortable accommodation for girls in Jaipur.

We understand the concerns of parents and the needs of young women who are away from home for education or work. Our mission is to provide a secure, homely environment where every resident feels safe, comfortable, and supported.

Over the years, we have hosted hundreds of satisfied residents from across India, building a reputation for excellence in safety, comfort, and care.

Our Mission & Vision

Committed to providing the best accommodation experience for girls

Our Mission

To provide safe, comfortable, and affordable accommodation for girls in Jaipur while creating a supportive community that feels like home. We strive to ensure every resident feels secure, valued, and empowered to achieve their goals.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and preferred girls' hostel in Jaipur, known for our commitment to safety, quality, and care. We envision a future where every girl has access to secure and comfortable accommodation that supports her dreams and aspirations.
